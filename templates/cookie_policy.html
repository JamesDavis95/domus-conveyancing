Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and analyzing how you use our services.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

  • Essential functionality: To enable core website features
  • Security: To protect against fraud and maintain security
  • Analytics: To understand how visitors use our website
  • Personalization: To remember your preferences and settings
  • Marketing: To show relevant advertisements (with your consent)

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They cannot be disabled.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
session_id Maintains your login session Session
csrf_token Security protection against cross-site attacks Session
auth_token Authentication and authorization 30 days
domus_cookie_consent Stores your cookie preferences 1 year

3.2 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Provider
_ga Distinguishes unique users 2 years Google Analytics
_ga_* Stores session state 2 years Google Analytics
_gid Distinguishes unique users 24 hours Google Analytics
_gat Throttles request rate 1 minute Google Analytics

3.3 Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used for advertising and marketing purposes.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Provider
_fbp Facebook browser tracking 3 months Facebook
_fbc Facebook click tracking 2 years Facebook
fr Facebook advertising delivery 3 months Facebook

3.4 Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your choices and personalize your experience.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
theme Remembers your theme preference (light/dark) 1 year
language Stores your language preference 1 year
dashboard_layout Remembers your dashboard layout preferences 6 months

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services we use:

4.1 Google Analytics

  • Purpose: Website analytics and performance monitoring
  • Data collected: Page views, session duration, user interactions
  • Privacy policy: Google Privacy Policy
  • Opt-out: Google Analytics Opt-out

4.2 Stripe

  • Purpose: Payment processing and fraud prevention
  • Data collected: Payment information, device fingerprinting
  • Privacy policy: Stripe Privacy Policy

4.3 Facebook Pixel

  • Purpose: Marketing and advertising optimization
  • Data collected: Website interactions, conversion tracking
  • Privacy policy: Meta Privacy Policy

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

5.1 Cookie Banner

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie banner where you can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Manage your preferences for different cookie categories

5.2 Preference Center

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by:

  • Clicking the "Cookie Preferences" link in our footer
  • Using the cookie management tool on any page
  • Visiting your account settings page

5.3 Browser Settings

You can also control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Advanced > Privacy and security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

6. International Transfers

Some of our third-party service providers may transfer your data internationally. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place:

  • Google Analytics: Processes data in accordance with GDPR
  • Stripe: EU-US Privacy Framework participant
  • Facebook: Standard Contractual Clauses for EU data

7. Data Retention

Cookie data is retained for different periods:

  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Deleted after their expiry date
  • Analytics data: Automatically deleted after 26 months
  • Consent records: Kept for 7 years for compliance purposes

8. Your Rights

Under GDPR and UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Withdraw consent: Change your cookie preferences at any time
  • Access: Request information about cookies we use
  • Erasure: Request deletion of cookie data
  • Portability: Receive your consent data in a machine-readable format
  • Complaint: Contact the Information Commissioner's Office

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Changes will be:

  • Posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Notified via email for significant changes
  • Prompted through our cookie banner for consent updates
